UTD Parking Permit Colors (and What They Allow)
🟢 Green Permit (cheapest commuter permit)

Access:

Green spaces in surface lots only

NOT allowed in parking garages

Located in the outer / perimeter lots

Notes

After 4:30–5 PM and weekends, restrictions loosen and you can often park in more areas depending on signage.

Designed for students willing to walk farther.

Typical green lots:

Lot U

Lot V

Parts of Lot A / B perimeter areas

Other outer campus surface lots

🟡 Gold Permit (mid-tier commuter permit)

Access:

Gold spaces in lots and garages

Many central campus lots

Parking structures allowed

Gold permits are allowed in:

Parking Structures PS1, PS3, PS4

Many central lots (C, D, H, etc.)

Gold is the most common commuter upgrade because it gets you closer parking and garage access.

🟠 Orange Permit (premium commuter permit)

Access:

Orange spaces

Best locations near academic buildings

All garages

Typical locations:

Prime spots near:

JSOM

Engineering buildings

Student Union

ATEC

These are the closest commuter spaces on campus.

🌙 Evening Orange Permit

Access:

Same spaces as Orange, but only after ~5 PM.

Good for:

Night classes

Part-time students

People arriving after normal daytime enforcement.

🟣 Purple Permit (faculty/staff)

Access:

Purple spaces

Best spots near buildings

Garages allowed

Primarily for faculty and staff, not typical student permits.

Residential Permits (different system)

If you live on campus you usually have one of these:

Residence Hall (red/white)

University Village

Canyon Creek Heights

These allow parking only in residential lots, not commuter areas.

Garage Rules

UTD garages generally allow these permits:

Permit	Garage Access
Green	❌ No
Gold	✅ Yes
Orange	✅ Yes
Purple	✅ Yes

Garages include:

PS1

PS3

PS4

These garages use colored lights above spaces matching permit colors.

Quick Hierarchy (Best → Worst parking)

Purple – Faculty premium

Orange – Closest student parking

Gold – Mid-tier + garages

Green – Far lots only

Think of it as pay more → park closer.
